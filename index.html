Shashank Gupta

Hello! I am Shashank Gupta. I am currently a Research Scientist at Rockwell Automation in Austin, TX. My research interests lie broadly in the areas of Computer Vision, Deep Learning, and Industry Automation.

In the past, I have worked as an AI Modelling Intern at KLA Tencor and as a Software Development Engineer at MathWorks Inc. I have also interned at Uber Lab in Computer Vision.

I graduated from the University of Michigan, Ann Arbor with a Master of Science in Computer Vision, Electrical and Computer Engineering. Prior to that, I completed my BTech in Electrical Engineering from Indian Institute of Technology Hyderabad, India.

Among other things, I enjoy implementing advanced machine learning models, working on challenging computer vision problems, and exploring the latest developments in AI and automation.
